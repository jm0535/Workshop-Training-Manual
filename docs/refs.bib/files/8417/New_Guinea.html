Cheklist of ants described and recorded from New Guinea and associated islands. Compiled by M. Janda, G. Alpert, M. Borowiec, P. Klimes, E. M. Sarnat, B. Guenard and S. Shattuck. The large island of New Guinea (the second largest island in the world) includes Indonesia (Western New Guinea) and Papua New Guinea (PNG). This checklist includes records from following areas of New Guinea region: New Guinea island; New Britain; New Ireland; Manus; Bismarck archipelago islands; Aru; d'Entrecasteaux islands; Lousiade archipelago; Waigeo; Misool.

This work was supported by Marie Curie Fellowship 'Ant Biodiversity' (PIOF-GA-2009-254448) and by Czech Science Foundation (P505/12/2467).
